Zing Child Safety Standards Policy

Zing is committed to maintaining a safe and respectful online environment for all users. We have a zero-tolerance policy for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). This page outlines our published standards as required by Google Play's Child Safety Standards policy.

1. Our Zero-Tolerance Policy

We strictly prohibit any content or activity that involves the sexual exploitation, abuse, or endangerment of minors. This includes any form of child sexual abuse material (CSAM), grooming, sextortion, or trafficking of minors. Any user found to be engaging in such activities will have their account permanently banned and may be reported to the relevant law enforcement authorities.

2. Reporting CSAE and CSAM

To help us keep the platform safe, we encourage users to report any suspected violations.

  • In-App Reporting: You can report any post, profile, or message directly within the Zing app by using the "Report" function, typically found by tapping the three-dot menu on a piece of content.
  • Direct Contact: For urgent matters related to child safety, you can contact our designated point of contact below.

3. Our Process for Addressing CSAM

We take all reports of potential child exploitation seriously. Upon receiving a report:

  • Our team will promptly investigate the content.
  • If content is confirmed to be CSAM, we will immediately remove it and permanently ban the responsible account.
  • We will report all instances of apparent CSAM to the National Center for Missing and Exploited Children (NCMEC) or other appropriate legal authorities, as required by law.

4. Compliance with Child Safety Laws

Zing is dedicated to complying with all applicable child safety and data privacy laws in the jurisdictions where we operate.

6. Additional Resources
